# Super-Store-Sales-Insights-Dashboard

## Project Objective
The Super Store Sales dataset provides detailed information about retail transactions across multiple regions and product categories. It includes data on sales, profit, quantity, discounts, customer segments, and order dates, enabling a deep analysis of business performance. 

## Dataset Used
<a href="https://github.com/Aravindkishore16/Super-Store-Insights-Dashboard/blob/main/SUPERSTORESALES1.xlsx">Dataset</a>

## Questions(KPIs)

-Which category contributes the most to overall revenue?

-Which product category generates the highest total sales?

-Which category earns the highest profit and which has the lowest?

-Are there any categories that generate high sales but low profit?

-Which sub-category has the highest sales volume?

-Which region records the highest total sales?

-How do sales compare between East, West, Central, and South regions?

Dashboard Interation <a href="https://github.com/Aravindkishore16/Super-Store-Insights-Dashboard/blob/main/Screenshot%20(92).png)">View Dashboard</a>

## Process

-Import and clean the Super Store dataset by removing duplicates and correcting data types.

-Create pivot tables for category-wise, sub-category-wise, region-wise, and segment-wise sales and profit.

-Calculate total sales, profit, and profit margin for each analysis type.

-Insert charts like bar, pie, and column charts to visualize performance comparisons.

-Combine all visuals into an interactive Excel dashboard using slicers for year, region, and category filters.

## Project Insights

-The Technology category records the highest sales and profit.

-Within sub-categories, Phones and Chairs are top revenue generators.

-The West region contributes the most to total sales, followed by the East region.

-The Consumer segment leads in overall sales volume, while the Corporate segment provides steady profit growth.

## Final Conclusion

-The analysis highlights that Technology is the most profitable and high-performing category overall.

-Sub-category insights show strong sales in Phones and Chairs, while Tables need pricing or discount adjustments.

-Regional performance reveals the West as the top-performing region, with opportunities for growth in the South.

-Customer segment analysis confirms the Consumer group as the primary revenue driver.

-Overall, focusing on profitable categories and improving low-margin areas can boost total sales and business efficiency.
